# Tee Marine APS

**Comprehensive Offshore Solutions**

TeeMarine delivers end-to-end offshore logistics solutions combining decades of experience in vessel management, project management, and executive consulting. From Arctic cargo ships to offshore supply vessels, anchor handlers, ERRVs, crew transfer vessels, walk-to-work vessels, and cable-laying ships—we operate a robust platform for rapid and efficient vessel integration under the Danish flag.

### AlphaDeck Platform
A simple, intuitive system for customers to view the vessels they hire, own, or manage. AlphaDeck provides transparency and easy access to critical vessel information:

- **Dashboard View** – See all your vessels at a glance with their current locations worldwide
- **Fleet View** – Extensive detailed information about each vessel you have access to
- **Certificates View** – Searchable access to all vessel certificates by certificate name or vessel name
- **Real-time Updates** – Stay informed about your fleet status and compliance

By providing direct access to vessel data and documentation, we empower our customers with the transparency and insights they need to make informed decisions quickly.
